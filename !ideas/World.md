##### World:

There will be the dungeon (crypt). This is a trial of sorts, once you enter, you cannot leave.

The crypt shape will be generated from a map, either predetermined or randomly, not sure yet.

There is a main village, which is where the shops will be.

Inside the crypt, there will be different sections. Each section has different types of enemies. Within the crypt, there will be different room types.



There will be 5 vaults, each with a boss you need to defeat to enter. The final vault, the boss is inside the vault.
